Privacy Policy – Daily Happiness

Effective: 26 Oct 2025

Overview

Daily Happiness is designed to be private by default. Your entries, tags, and photos are stored locally on your iPhone and are not uploaded to our servers.

Data We Collect

  • None. We do not collect personal data, analytics, or usage statistics.

Data on Your Device

  • Entries, tags, and photos are stored on-device only.
  • Photos/Camera access is used solely when you attach images to an entry.

Tracking, Ads, and Analytics

No tracking, no third‑party ads, and no analytics SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Because data remains on your device, please keep your device secure (passcode, Face ID, backups).

Contact

Email: post2tony@gmail.com
